张小蕾 Xiaolei Zhang

I am passionate about advancing healthcare using computational and statistical approaches. I have studied Computational Biology and Bioinformatics in ETH Zurich. I am finishing my PhD in Imperial College London in the study of statistical methods for clinical genome interpretation with specific application in inherited cardiac conditions.
